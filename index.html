Rotary Medical Store

Rotary Medical Store

Rotary Medical Store was opened on 08th of Dec 2020, having a view of providing day to day medicines at a discounted price to the patients. The project was started with an Idea of our Past President Rtn. Piyush Ajmera. A complete workout was done on this project and then it was presented to all members. The requirement of initial amount of INR 5.50 Lakhs was gathered from all club members as a refundable non interest one year loan to club. All members were agreed to this condition and the amount was fulfilled in no time. The grand opening was done and reputed peoples from various fields of the town were invited in this opening ceremony. This Medical store is now having a great image in the society where patients get benefited by discounted price medicines. As of now this store is having a 40 thousand of recurring expense on monthly basis of which a part is fulfilled by profit of sales of medicines and mostly by members who keeps on fulfilling the required amount. We are still seeking for the Donors to bear the monthly cost of the project. Club members Rtn. Dr. Manish Vakotar & Rtn. Nirav Zinzuvadia looks after the records of this Store. This medical Store is strengthening clubs public image in the society.

Bal Vatsalya Kendra

Rotary Bal Vatsalya Kendra

Our club has started a Bal Vatsalya Kendra in Amreli. Basically it’s a child development & educational training centre for rural & slum area children. This project is started after a motivational visit of Happy School of RC Rajkot Greater and Dhinka Chika project by Surat during Pravas - Car Rally organized by RID 3060. We identified slum areas in the city and surveyed for the childrens in this areas. We approached many institutions for a hand of help and luckily SBI – RSETI has agreed to help us and provided us the place in their institute. We arraigned a care taker for this childrens and started this centre on 22nd of Feb 2021. We are glad to see that on the very 1st day of this centre, nearly 35 childrens were present and this figure is now increasing day by day. As of now there are 109 childrens coming to this centre. Care taker trains these childrens on how to Brush teeth, taking Bath, Combing their Hair & Dressing, etc. Club member Rtn. Suresh Trivedi & Rtn. Tapan Sanghvi looks after this project and also teaches the childrens basic knowledge. This centre is open daily from 8:30 to 11:30 in morning and a healthy meal is giving to all children which attract them for coming here. Our aim is to educate maximum children of slum areas. Recurring cost of around Rs.6000-8000/- is barred by club members and SBI-RSETI. By this project club is gaining public image in the lower class of the society.

Rotary Specialty Clinic

Rotary Specialty Clinic

Rotary Specialty Clinic has been started with a motto to serve public specially lower - middle class people in their medical treatment at reasonable prices. This clinic serves patients in specialty treatments like Dental Care, Skin & Cosmetic Care, Orthopaedic, Eye Care and also in General check-ups. All the treatments are done by expertise doctors with a relief of 70-80% amount in treatment cost. The amount of starting this clinic was estimated for 80-90 Thousands and was fulfilled by club members in a short time. A donor agrees to give his shop on much lower rental basis and work to start the clinic begins. Meetings with all Doctors and working conditions were finalized and staff was recruited. Clinic was opened for public on 23rd of January 2021. This Clinic is now serving about 40-45 patients on daily basis and more than 2500 patients have been benefited till date saving an aprrox amount of Rs. 3.50 lakhs of the patients. Free medical check-up camps are also done once a week in this clinic. This Clinic is located near Rotary Medical Store so it also helps in boosting the sales of medical store where patients gets the medicines at a discounted price, thus making a big effect on cost of treatment and medicines to patients. The recurring cost of this clinic including advertisement and other expenses is nearly Rs.25,000/- on monthly basis and the same by sharing consulting fees of doctors and remaining amount is fulfilled by club members. Club member Rtn. Hardik Zinzuvadia & Rtn. Dr. Bhavesh Mehta manages this project & maintains the records. This Rotary Specialty Clinic is gaining public attraction day by day thus helping to build a better public image of the club in society.

Project Aadhar

Project Aadhar

Project Aadhar is a project in which our club provides Medical Equipments to patients on a refundable deposit basis. This project was started on the Charter Installation of Club as a thought to serve the society from the very 1st day of the club. On an initial stage we filtered Medical Equipments like Air Bed, Water Bed, Toilet Chair, Walking Stand & Sticks, Medical Beds, ect. and approached many local donors to donate for this cause. The total amount of these equipments was around 1.50 Lakhs INR. Fortunately in a very short period of time our Donors agreed to give a handsome amount for this project. Club members fulfilled the remaining part of the amount and the project we started. The whole project is managed by club member Rtn. Trilok Bhatt and all the equipments are kept at Deaf & Dumb School – Amreli. The patient / beneficiary are asked to pay a deposit amount depending on the equipment needed & on the returning the equipment the deposit amount is refunded to the beneficiary. A complete record book is maintained for this project. Many beneficiaries also donated the deposit amount which helps in the recurring cost of the project. Many other local donors keeps on helping this project as a result of which, this project is now having Medical Equipments of more than 5.75 Lakhs including two oxygen concentrate machines. This project is now having a vast effect on society and benefiting more than 6,000 beneficiaries till date thus helping the club build a better Public Image in the society

Oxygen Cylinder Bank

Oxygen Cylinder Bank

"Oxygen Cylinder Bank" this project which our club provides oxygen cylinders to patients on the basis of a refundable deposit. The entire project is managed by club member RTN. Trilok Bhatt and all Cylinders are kept at Mukbadhir School - Amreli. The project was launched on the second wave of the Covid epidemic. A complete record book is kept for this project. Many beneficiaries also donated deposit amounts to help with recurring costs of the project. Many other local donors continue to help the project, as a result, the project now has more than 65 Oxygen Cylinders. The project is now having a wider impact on the community and has benefited more than 6,000 beneficiaries to date thus helping the club to create a better public image in the community.

Miyawaki Forests

Miyawaki Forests

Club has developed 2 Miyawaki Forests of 100 Sq. Mtr. each at Deaf & Dumb School Amreli. This method used for tree plantation is in self an innovative way and by this method we can plant more trees in a small area compared to our traditional planting method. While in this Miyawaki Forest method approx 350 trees can be planted in 100 Sq. Mt. areas besides approx 80-100 trees by traditional method thus helping Rotarys new area of focus Environment. Among this, the plantation will be self sustainable after a period of 12 to 18 months where its takes a lot of efforts, precious care and time consuming in other methods. In this method we can select trees and plants depending on surrounding environment thus helping in creating a better place to live for birds.
